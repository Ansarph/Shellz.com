FAQ

Which is better, Bluehost or SiteGround?

The better choice depends on your website. Bluehost is strongest for beginner wordpress sites and small businesses, while SiteGround is strongest for managed wordpress tools and performance-focused users.

Should I choose the cheapest intro price?

Not by itself. Compare renewal rates, backups, support, storage, email, domain renewal, migration, and your expected traffic before choosing.
