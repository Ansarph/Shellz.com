Can you really start hosting under $25?

Yes, but usually only for a starter setup. The budget may cover a discounted first billing term, a very basic hosting plan, or a cheap domain plus basic hosting promotion. It may not cover professional email, daily backups, advanced security, premium themes, privacy tools, or long-term renewals.

What to avoid

  • Buying a long contract only because the first price looks low.
  • Ignoring renewal pricing.
  • Adding paid backups, security, privacy, email, or SEO tools without checking whether you need them.
  • Assuming a coupon works before testing it at checkout.
  • Choosing a host only by first-year price when you plan to keep the site for years.
