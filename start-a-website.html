Step 1: Choose your website type

Before you buy hosting, decide whether you are building a blog, local business website, affiliate website, ecommerce store, portfolio, or SaaS landing page. This controls how much storage, speed, support, and upgrade capacity you need.

Step 2: Pick a hosting category

Shared hosting

Step 3: Use the checkout checklist

  • Total due today
  • Renewal price after the intro term
  • Free domain duration and renewal price
  • SSL certificate included or paid
  • Email inboxes included or extra
  • Backups and restore options
  • Migration support
  • Refund window and cancellation rules

FAQ

What is the cheapest way to start a website?

Usually a shared hosting or beginner WordPress hosting plan is the lowest-cost path for a custom-domain website. Compare the renewal price before buying.

Should beginners use WordPress?

WordPress is a strong choice for blogs, affiliate sites, service-business sites, and content-heavy projects. Website builders can be easier for very simple pages.
