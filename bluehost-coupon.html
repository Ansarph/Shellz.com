FAQ

Does Bluehost have a coupon code?

What should I check before using a Bluehost discount?

Check the total due today, renewal price, term length, included domain or email, backup features, refund rules, and whether the offer applies to the exact plan you want.

Are hosting coupons always the cheapest option?

Not always. A plan with a bigger first-term discount can cost more after renewal. Compare the total first term and expected renewal cost before choosing.
