# Shellz New-Gen Hosting Monthly Update Checklist

This is an internal editorial checklist. Do not publish this as a visitor-facing section on `new-gen-hosting.html`.

## Monthly update steps

1. Check official provider pages for current pricing, features, promos, coupons, refund notes, support notes, and plan changes.
2. Update the New-Gen Hosting provider list if a provider became more or less relevant.
3. Confirm coupon status:
   - Real coupon code
   - Auto-applied deal
   - No public coupon code verified
4. Add or update one detailed E-E-A-T review.
5. Add or update one comparison page.
6. Check internal links from:
   - Homepage
   - Reviews page
   - Promo Codes page
   - New-Gen Hosting page
   - Related guide pages
7. Update `sitemap.xml`.
8. Submit important new or updated URLs in Google Search Console.
9. Track impressions, clicks, CTR, and average position.
10. Do not invent prices, coupon codes, refund terms, uptime claims, or support claims.
