What is Cloudways?

Cloudways is a managed cloud hosting platform that sits atop major Infrastructure-as-a-Service providers such as DigitalOcean, AWS, Google Cloud, Vultr, and Linode. Unlike traditional shared web hosting, Cloudways focuses on performance, flexibility, and ease of use for developers, agencies, and businesses needing scalable and reliable managed cloud servers.

Who is Cloudways Best For?

  • Web Agencies & Freelancers: Who need to manage multiple client sites with simplicity and power.
  • Developers & Technical Users: Who want full server control without direct infrastructure management.
  • Businesses & Ecommerce: That need scalable, reliable, performance-oriented hosting without managing servers themselves.
  • Anyone Outgrowing Shared Hosting and wanting a managed, cloud-based upgrade.

Who Should Avoid Cloudways?

  • Non-Technical Beginners: Cloudways is easier than raw cloud computing, but still expects some tech knowledge (like server/app management).
  • Small, Budget-Conscious Sites: Shared hosting or cheaper managed WordPress hosts may better suit those with basic requirements.
  • Needs for Email Hosting: Cloudways does not provide built-in email hosting, only SMTP add-ons; you'll need a 3rd-party mail provider.

Final Verdict: Is Cloudways Worth It?

Cloudways is one of the top managed cloud hosting solutions if you need performance, flexibility, and a hands-off server management experience. Their unique offering gives you infrastructure choice (DigitalOcean, AWS, GCP, etc.), managed security, easy scaling, and fast global deployment. While it’s not truly “no code” like some beginner hosts, it is much easier than traditional cloud setup. Their pay-as-you-go billing is transparent and the included features (SSL, backups, support, staging) make Cloudways a reliable bridge between basic shared hosting and enterprise-grade cloud solutions.

If you’re running mission-critical websites, e-commerce platforms, multiple client projects, or high-traffic applications and have some technical confidence, Cloudways is highly recommended in 2024.

Cloudways FAQ

Does Cloudways include email hosting?
No, Cloudways does not provide standard email inboxes with hosting. You can add email via paid 3rd-party add-ons or external providers.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
No, Cloudways operates a post-paid/pay-as-you-go model and does not offer standard money-back guarantees. See the official refund policy above.
How does Cloudways handle security (SSL, updates)?
All plans include free auto-renewing SSL and managed OS/stack updates (provider-claimed).
Does Cloudways offer staging and backups?
Yes, all plans include one-click staging, daily automated backups, and on-demand manual backups.
Can I migrate my existing site?
Cloudways offers a free WordPress migration plugin, and paid migration for other platforms.
