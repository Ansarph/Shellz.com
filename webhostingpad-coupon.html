Official FREEDOMAIN terms

  • Available with an eligible new hosting account.
  • Provides one year of eligible domain registration.
  • The current official hosting terms describe a maximum value of $16.99.
  • Higher-priced or premium domains may require payment of the difference.
  • The offer must be claimed within the first 30 days of account signup.
  • It may be claimed only once per account.
  • The domain renews later at the regular rate.
  • The code may not combine with sale pricing or another coupon.
  • Domain charges are generally excluded from the hosting money-back guarantee.

How to use the coupon

  1. Open the Shellz WebHostingPad affiliate link.
  2. Select a new eligible hosting plan.
  3. Add an eligible domain to the order.
  4. Enter FREEDOMAIN if a coupon field is available.
  5. Check whether the code removes or reduces the domain charge.
  6. Compare the result against any automatically applied hosting sale.
  7. Review the hosting renewal price and future domain renewal price.
  8. Save a screenshot of the final checkout total.

Should you use the coupon or the sale price?

WebHostingPad's billing policy says a sale price offered through a coupon may not be combined with another coupon, including the free-domain offer. The best choice depends on the final dollar value. A hosting discount can sometimes save more than a free first-year domain.

Calculate both possible totals when the checkout allows it. Use the option that produces the lower verified cost and still fits your hosting needs.
